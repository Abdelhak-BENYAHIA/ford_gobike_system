Investigation Overview¶

we are going to investigate Ford GoBike System we are going to find out when (start time, ride duration..) the system is mostly used and by whom (membership, gender ,age) is used the most. Also we are going to find out if there is a relationship between user type and other features.

Dataset Overview¶

This dataset includes information about FORD GOBIKE SYSTEM trips made in february 2019 in the San Francisco Bay area by. At start the dataset had 183412 trip record with 16 features classified into three gategories. category 1: ride time ( date, start time, end time, ride duration..), category 2 : ride stations ( start station , end station) and category 3: rider ( membership, gender ,age)

Rider informations¶

  • The system is mostly used by subscribers (90%)
  • The system is mostly used by people aged between 20 and 40 especially between 25 and 35.

User type relation to trip duration¶

subscribers tend to make short trips (no longer than 10 min) while customers tend to have longer trips

User Type and time usage¶

Subscribers tend to use the system in the rush hours 8 am and 5 pm and in working days, while customers trips are almost equally distributed all day long and all days of the week but they tend to use it especially in the weekends.

Bike most used¶

Some of the bike are used more than others. Some of the bike may be located at places frequented mostly by susbcribers who uses the system for work related reasons. Top 2 bikes were used mostly in rush hours and less used in weekends
